# Personalization

Aventir adapts to your unique needs and workflow preferences.

## User Profile

Store your name, role, company, and preferences.

## Communication Style

Choose tone: Professional, Casual, or Concise.

## Notification Preferences

Configure channels, frequency, and content types.

## Default Behaviors

Set auto-approval thresholds and scheduling defaults.

## Custom Instructions

Global and per-agent instructions for consistent output.

## Privacy Controls

Manage data retention, access, and export options.
