# Proactive Features

Aventir learns your patterns and proactively suggests optimizations.

## Pattern Recognition

Detects when you check results, create agents, and review reports.

## Predictive Scheduling

Suggests optimal timing based on your activity patterns.

## Anomaly Detection

Alerts on unusual patterns:
- Credit usage spikes
- Success rate drops
- Schedule misses

## Contextual Suggestions

Recommends actions based on context:
- Pre-meeting briefings
- Post-travel catch-ups
- End-of-day summaries

## Smart Notifications

Priority-based routing with quiet hours support.
