## What are Credits?

Credits are the currency for AI operations within Aventir:

- **Simple task** (email check, notification): 1-2 credits
- **Standard task** (data sync, form fill): 3-5 credits
- **Complex task** (research, analysis): 6-10 credits
- **Virtual computer** (hourly): 20 credits/hour

Unused credits roll over for 30 days.
