---
title: "Notion Integration"
description: "Automate Notion workspaces with Aventir agents"
---

Connect Aventir to Notion to create pages, update databases, and manage knowledge.

## Setup

1. In Notion, go to page you want to automate
2. Click **Share** → **Add integration** → **Aventir**
3. Set permissions (Read, Write, or Full access)
4. In Aventir, go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Notion**
5. Complete OAuth flow

## Capabilities

- Create and update pages
- Query and modify databases
- Extract content from documents
- Organize workspace structure

## Workflows

- Task management from emails
- Meeting notes organization
- Documentation sync
- CRM integration

## Rate Limits

3-100+ requests/second depending on Notion plan.
