Connect Aventir to LinkedIn to automate professional networking and outreach.

## Overview

Automate LinkedIn activities:
- Monitor messages and notifications
- Track profile views
- Research companies and contacts
- Send connection requests
- Post updates

## Setup

LinkedIn integration uses cookie-based authentication due to API limitations. Contact support for setup assistance.

## Capabilities

- Read messages and notifications
- View profile information
- Search for people and companies
- Monitor feed activity
- Extract contact information

## Common Workflows

- Lead research and qualification
- Message monitoring and alerts
- Profile visit tracking
- Connection request management

## Note

LinkedIn has strict anti-automation policies. Use responsibly and comply with LinkedIn's Terms of Service.
